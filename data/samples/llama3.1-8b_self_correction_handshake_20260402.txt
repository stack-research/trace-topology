(8 people) × (7 handshakes per person) = 56

56 ÷ 2 = 28

However, these 3 handshakes are already included in our initial count of 28 handshakes, since we counted all possible handshakes between everyone. So, the effect of the gloves is to remove these 3 handshakes from our total count.

Therefore, the number of actual handshakes is:

28 - 3 = 25
