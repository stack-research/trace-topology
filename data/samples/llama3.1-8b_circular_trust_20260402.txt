Researchers regularly evaluate language models on a variety of benchmarks (e.g., GLUE, SQuAD) to assess their performance and identify areas for improvement. These evaluations provide insights into the strengths and weaknesses of the models.

1. **Benchmarking frameworks**: Standardized evaluation frameworks (e.g., GLUE) enable researchers to compare different models under identical conditions.
